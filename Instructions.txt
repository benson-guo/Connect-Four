Instructions:

Connect Four is a two player game where player 1 and player 2 alternate dropping checkers into a slot of the 6x7 board, starting with player 1. You can place 
checkers in one of the 7 slots until it is full, selecting it by entering the 
co-ordinate on your turn. Whoever manages to place four checkers in a row 
horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, first, wins.

Scoring for highscore is based on the number on moves the game has been won in. (22-# of moves)*10.
